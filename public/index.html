One Venue. Infinite Journeys.

A stadium isn't just one space; it's a city within a city, with thousands of people needing different things at the same time. A generic WiFi network sees chaos. Our AI Gateway sees a symphony and acts as the conductor.

Stadiums & Event Spaces

The Problem: You have tens of thousands of people—fans, vendors, security, artists—all with different needs, permissions, and objectives. Disconnected systems for ticketing, POS, and security create friction, missed revenue, and operational blind spots.

The Gateway Solution: It identifies, authenticates, and personalizes the journey for everyone. Let's follow a few people at today's championship game:

Meet Chloe, The Fan.

Upon connecting, the Gateway authenticates her ticket. Her phone becomes her pass, wallet, and guide. She pre-orders drinks to skip the line, uses the AR wayfinding to locate her friends, and receives an exclusive 20% discount on merchandise for her favorite player, triggered the moment she walks past the team store.

Meet Marcus, The Vendor.

The Gateway is his business intelligence partner. It connects his POS to the stadium's inventory system. Based on real-time footfall data and historical trends, it alerts him he's projected to sell out of hot dogs in 30 minutes and automatically suggests a restock order. All transactions are cashless and instantly reconciled.

Meet David, Security Ops.

He has a God's-eye view. The Gateway identifies him as security staff, giving his device secure access to internal camera feeds and staff communication channels. Heatmap data shows unexpected congestion forming at Gate 7; he dispatches a team to the precise location before it becomes a problem, all from his tablet.

Meet Anya, The Sound Tech.

When Anya connects, the Gateway recognizes her device's MAC address and authenticates her as part of the production crew. It automatically grants her access to the isolated, high-bandwidth production network, allowing her to securely monitor and control audio systems across the venue wirelessly, without interference from the public network.
