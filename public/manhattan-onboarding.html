Phase 2: Technical Foundation

Here, we configure the core technical components based on the information provided in your templates. This involves setting up your portal structure and integrating your Unifi network.

Step 2.1: Portal Structure Setup

Using the details from your "Groups & Venues" template, we will configure the portal with the following hierarchy for granular control and reporting:

Main Account:
  • Manhattan Hotel
Groups:
  • Public Areas
  • Restaurants & Bars
  • Guest Rooms
  • Conference Rooms
  • Admin & BoH
Venues:
  • Lobby & Reception
  • Restaurant & Spa
  • Conference Rooms 1-3
  • Guest Floors 1-10
  • Admin Offices

Step 2.2: Unifi Hardware Integration

We will liaise directly with your IT provider to connect your Unifi Access Points. This involves simple configuration changes on the Unifi controller, with no physical hardware changes needed.

Phase 3: Crafting the Guest Journey

We will design the entire visual and interactive journey for your guests, incorporating a strategic mix of login methods to balance convenience with valuable data capture.

Guest & Staff Login Options

We will enable the following login methods, each serving a distinct purpose:

  • Registration Form: The primary method for capturing high-quality, opt-in guest data like names and email addresses for marketing.
  • Facebook: A popular, low-friction option for guests that provides valuable demographic insights.
  • LinkedIn: An ideal choice for conference and business guests, offering professional network data.
  • X (Twitter): Provides another quick social login option for guests.
  • Sponsored WiFi: Allows temporary, branded access for conferences or events, creating a revenue opportunity.
  • SAML/Staff Login: A secure method for employees to access a separate staff network using their company credentials (configured in Phase 4).

Secure Staff WiFi (SAML)

We will configure a separate, secure WiFi network (SSID) exclusively for staff. Using SAML authentication, employees can log in with their existing company credentials. This keeps internal traffic separate from the guest network, enhancing security and providing a better experience for everyone.
